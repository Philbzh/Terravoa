The Liquid Gold of
The Maremma

"The secret isn't in the press, it's in the patience. We wait for the first frost to kiss the Frantoio trees before we even think of the harvest."

In the heart of Tuscany, Matteo Rossi continues a tradition that spans four centuries. His 'Savoir-Faire' lies in the artisanal cold-pressing techniques that preserve the volatile aromas of the earth. We spent a week in his groves to bring you this limited harvest.
